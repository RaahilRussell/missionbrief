# Youth Development Grant Agreement

**Grantee:** BrightPath Youth Center
**Funder:** Cedar Valley Community Fund
**Grant Amount:** $25,000
**Grant Period:** January 1, 2026 – December 31, 2026
**Program Officer:** R. Delgado
**Agreement ID:** CVCF-2026-0142

## Purpose

This grant supports BrightPath Youth Center's Youth Development Program, including
after-school programming, STEM workshops, mentoring, and family support services for
youth ages 11–18 in the Northgate and Westside neighborhoods.

## Grant Terms

- Total award: **$25,000**, disbursed in two tranches.
- Funds are restricted to youth development programming and may not be used for
  capital expenses or fundraising costs.
- Quarterly outcome reports are due within 30 days of each quarter close.
- A year-end impact evaluation is required by December 15, 2026.
