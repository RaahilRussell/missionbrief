# BrightPath Youth Center — Board Packet (April 2026)

*Prepared for the Board of Directors meeting on May 6, 2026.*

## Executive Summary

April was a strong month for BrightPath. We served 198 unique youth across our four
programs and launched the spring STEM workshop cohort. Program completion held at 84%.
The organization remains on budget, with the Cedar Valley youth development grant 56%
utilized at month end.

## Program Highlights

- Unique youth served: 198
- Average after-school attendance: 41 youth per session
- STEM spring cohort launched with 32 enrolled
- Mentoring match rate: 90%

## Financial Snapshot

- Youth development grant spent to date: $14,050 of $25,000 (56%)
- Operating budget on track; no line items over budget.

## Grant Status

- Q1 outcomes report submitted to Cedar Valley on April 28 (milestone complete).
- 200-youth enrollment milestone achieved.

## Risks / Blockers

- Recruiting for the bilingual family support coordinator was behind schedule.
- After-school attendance trending toward room capacity.

## Board Decisions

- Approved summer programming budget.
- Deferred decision on after-school capacity to the May meeting.
